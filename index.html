About Us

Ketchup from COVID19 Pandemic with us!

At Covid Ketchup, we aim to aid young students in their learning journeys. Due to COVID-19, many students were set behind in their curriculum because of a sudden shift to distance learning. As such, our team created and compiled a curriculum for each grade from Kindergarten to 5th grade, in order to help students catch back up to where they would otherwise be. Through fun and interactive worksheets, and a future point based system where students can be motivated to participate daily, we intend to create the most efficient and exciting learning environment for our participants.
